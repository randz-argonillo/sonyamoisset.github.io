About Me

A life of travels and experiences

My name is Sonya Moisset. I have lived in the Middle-East, North Africa and Asia most of my life, always looking for new challenges. In 2011, I moved to South Korea from Singapore, learned Korean, and have settled in Seoul for the time being. At the moment, I am working as a business consultant and a freelance reporter.

Technology and computers have been in my life since I was a kid and I always loved anything related with arts or pixels so I started to code and design at a very young age. Most of my skills and knowledge are self-taught.

I am curious, and I enjoy work that challenges me to learn something new and stretch in a different direction. I have a passion for designing detailed, creative and modern websites or graphics. I am constantly keeping an eye on the latest trends over technologies, languages, trends, etc.

I always do my best to stay on top of changes in the state of this industry so that I can meet challenges with tools well suited to the job at hand.

I aspire to become a complete full stack web developer.

I have lived and worked in more than 15 countries. I am fluent in English, French, Spanish and Korean.
